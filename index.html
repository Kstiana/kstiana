About Me

Crafting the Web,

I'm Christiana, a frontend developer with a passion for building interfaces that are as performant as they are beautiful. I believe great code and great design are inseparable — every project I take on is a chance to push both forward.

From AI-powered chat UIs to immersive skincare brand sites, I bring products to life with clean HTML, expressive CSS, and purposeful JavaScript. I obsess over the details — animations that feel natural, layouts that breathe, and interactions that delight.
